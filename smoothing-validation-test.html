Step 5: Validation Checklist

Check each item as you observe it:

  • Console shows animation start/end logs during menu toggle
  • Logs show 1500ms for PnLChart and PerformanceTrendChart
  • Logs show 1800ms for EmotionRadar
  • Transitions feel artificially smooth (like slow-motion)
  • Charts take 1.5-1.8 seconds to complete transitions
  • Multiple charts animating creates compounded smoothing effect

Step 6: Assessment

If few or no items are checked: We need to investigate other causes.
